"Raya and the Last Dragon" is more than just an animated adventure; it's a timeless story about trust, unity, and the power of believing in a better tomorrow. Its captivating characters, rich world, and profound themes make it a film that resonates with audiences of all ages.

TRIBE

Fang


The Fang Tribe is known for being fierce assassins and having an affinity for cats, Fang used the effects of the Druun's return for their own prosperity. With a skilled army, Fang is meant to have a militaristic feel.

TRIBE

Heart


Heart is said to represent the heart of a dragon with its lush rainforests and thriving state. It is unknown what happened to the people of Heart after the Dragon Gem was broken into separate pieces.

TRIBE

Spine


The Spine Tribe, though a shadow of their former glory,
remains a captivating element in the world of Raya and the Last Dragon. Their stoic grace, martial prowess, and connection to the natural world add a layer of depth and intrigue to the film's narrative.

TRIBE

Talon


The land of Talon as a floating market, and the village is exactly that when she arrives in the region in the hopes of attaining Chieftess Dang Hu's gem piece. The citizens of Talon wear purple and are known for having fast hands.

TRIBE

Tail


The people of Tail live in scattered settlements. They are hardy folk who survive by reusing and recycling. Nothing goes to waste in Tail. It is unknown who is the current chief of Tail.

MEET

RAYA

Raya, the spirited warrior princess from Disney's "Raya and the Last Dragon," is a heroine
who steals hearts with her determination, resourcefulness, and fierce loyalty.
Raya is a skilled fighter, trained in swordsmanship and hand-to-hand combat.
Her fighting style blends various Southeast Asian martial arts,
like the Filipino martial art of Kali and the Indonesian martial art of Pencak Silat.

MEET

SISU

Sisu is characterized by her optimism, humor, and infectious enthusiasm. She is often
described as being "bubbly" and "ditzy," but she is also wise, resourceful, and deeply
caring. Sisu is a firm believer in the power of trust and teamwork, and she encourages
Raya to open up to others and rely on their help to achieve her goals.

MEET

CHIEF BENJA

Chief Benja Known as “the baddest blade in the five lands,”
Benja is Raya’s beloved father and legendary Guardian of the Dragon Gem.
As chief of Heart, he’s an idealistic and bold visionary who believes
that Kumandra is broken because the people of the different lands don’t
trust each other. He seeks to reunite the fractured kingdom of Kumandra
and restore harmony.

MEET

NAMAARI

Namaari Brilliant, calculating, and a formidable warrior,
Namaari is Raya’s unrelenting nemesis.
She is the daughter of the Chief of the Fang lands and is determined to do
whatever it takes to protect her people.
However, down deep, she holds a secret love for dragons.

MEET

BUON

Boun A precocious street kid from Tail who’s always up for a business deal,
Boun is an adult in a child’s body—capable and self-sufficient.
He’s the self-styled “owner, manager, chef, and captain” of his boat, the Shrimporium.
Deep down, he’s a vulnerable child who lost his parents to the Druun.
